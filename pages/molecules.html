Molecules

Molecules are groups of atoms bonded together that take on distinct new properties. In interfaces, molecules are relatively simple groups of UI elements functioning together as a unit. Forr example. a form label, a search input, and a button can join together to create a search form molecule.

When combined, these abstract atoms sudenly have a purpose. The resul is a simple, portable, reusable component that can be dropped in anywhere search functionality is needed.
